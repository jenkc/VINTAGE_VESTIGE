# Weeks 7-8: Growth & First Revenue
**Vintage Vestige Build Plan**

**Focus:** User acquisition and revenue validation  
**Time Commitment:** 80 hours total (40/week)

---

## 🎯 Mission

**Get serious about growth and make first dollar.**

Week 7: Scale to 2,000 users OR 20 sellers in trial  
Week 8: Generate first revenue ($87-290 MRR)

---

## Week 7: User Acquisition Push

### If Consumer is Focus:

**Daily Tasks:**
- Reddit: Post in 2 vintage communities daily
- Instagram: Create @vintagevestige account, daily vintage finds
- Content: Publish 2 blog posts this week
- Partnerships: Reach out to 5 micro-influencers

**Goal:** 2,000 total users, 200 weekly active

**Tactics:**
- "How to find [specific style]" educational content
- Share interesting finds from search engine
- Engage authentically in communities
- Behind-the-scenes of building product

### If Seller is Focus:

**Daily Tasks:**
- Email 10 new sellers daily (50 total this week)
- Follow up with Week 5 demo participants
- Offer extended free trials
- Collect testimonials from happy users

**Goal:** 20 sellers actively using free version

**Tactics:**
- Personalized outreach (reference their shop specifically)
- "I noticed you sell [70s dresses], built a tool to help you list faster"
- Offer to do free setup call
- Show time savings: "10 min per listing → 30 sec"

---

## Week 8: Revenue Validation

### If Consumer is Focus:

**Monetization Tests:**
- Implement premium tier ($5/month):
  - Unlimited saved searches
  - Email notifications for new matches
  - Early access to features
- Optimize affiliate links
- Test: Will anyone actually pay?

**Goal:** $50-100 in combined revenue (affiliate + premium)

### If Seller is Focus:

**Sales Push:**
- Email all free trial users (Week 7 + previous)
- Message: "Your trial ends this week. Continue for $29/month?"
- Offer: First 10 customers get $19/month forever
- Personal outreach via email/DM

**Goal:** 3-10 paying customers ($87-290 MRR)

**Critical success:** Even 3 paying customers proves this works

## 💰 First Revenue Milestone

**When you get your first paying customer:**

1. Screenshot the Stripe notification
2. Post on Twitter: "First customer! 🎉"
3. Thank them personally
4. Ask for testimonial
5. **Celebrate** - this validates everything

**First dollar is the hardest. After that, it's about scale.**

## 💪 Motivation

### When Growth Feels Slow:
> "Compound growth starts small. Day 1: 10 users. Day 30: 1,000 users."

### When No One Pays:
> "Price might be wrong, positioning might be off, or it's the wrong product. Learn and adjust."

### When You Compare to Others:
> "They had years. You have weeks. You're doing amazing."

**These two weeks prove whether this becomes a business or a learning experience.**
