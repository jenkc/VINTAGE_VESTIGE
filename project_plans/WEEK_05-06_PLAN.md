# Weeks 5-6: Validation & Iteration
**Vintage Vestige Build Plan**

**Focus:** Deep user feedback and product improvements  
**Time Commitment:** 80 hours total (40/week)

---

## 🎯 Mission

**Understand what users actually want and build it.**

Week 5: Talk to 25 users (15 consumers, 10 sellers)  
Week 6: Fix critical issues and ship most-requested features

---

## Week 5: User Interviews & Validation

### Monday-Wednesday: Consumer Interviews (15 total)
**Schedule 15-minute Zoom calls with users from Week 3 launch**

**Interview Script:**
1. Watch them use the product (5 min)
2. What were you hoping to find? (2 min)
3. Was it helpful? What was confusing? (3 min)
4. How does this compare to how you normally shop? (2 min)
5. Would you use this regularly? Pay for premium? (3 min)

**Track:**
- What worked well
- What confused them
- Feature requests
- Would they pay? How much?

### Thursday-Friday: Seller Demos (10 total)
**Watch sellers upload their products and use AI tagging**

**Demo Script:**
1. Show example (upload dress, AI generates tags) (3 min)
2. Have them upload 3-5 of their products (10 min)
3. Discuss: Does this save time? Is AI accurate? (5 min)
4. **Ask: "Would you pay $29/month?"** (2 min)
5. What other features would make this worth paying for? (2 min)

**Critical Validation:**
- How many say yes to $29/month?
- What's their ideal price?
- What features are must-haves?

### Weekend: Analysis
**Compile all feedback:**
- Consumer patterns (common requests, pain points)
- Seller patterns (pricing sensitivity, feature needs)
- Decide: Focus on consumer or seller? Or both?

---

## Week 6: Build What Users Want

### Monday-Tuesday: Fix Critical Bugs
**From Week 5 feedback:**
- Top 3 consumer complaints → fix
- Top 3 seller issues → fix
- Deploy fixes to production

### Wednesday-Thursday: Ship Most-Requested Feature

**If consumers requested:**
- Saved favorites (localStorage or accounts)
- Saved searches with notifications
- Size filters
- Better mobile UX

**If sellers requested:**
- Cross-posting to multiple platforms
- Bulk CSV improvements
- Pricing intelligence
- Better AI accuracy

**Pick ONE feature. Ship it well.**

### Friday: Beta User Communication
- Email all beta users about improvements
- "Based on your feedback, we added X"
- Re-engage them to test new features

### Weekend: Content & Growth
- Write blog post: "How to [solve problem product solves]"
- SEO optimization
- Social media content
- Small growth experiments

## 💪 Motivation

### When Feedback is Harsh:
> "Users who care enough to criticize care enough to pay. This is valuable."

### When You're Unsure Which Product to Build:
> "The market will tell you. Listen to who's most excited."

### When 50% of Sellers Say No to $29:
> "That's not failure. That's learning. Adjust and try again."

**These two weeks determine your strategy for the final month.**
