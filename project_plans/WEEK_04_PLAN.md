## 💰 Pricing Strategy

**Free Tier:**
- 10 AI analyses per month
- Manual export (copy/paste)
- Attracts users, collects data

**Paid Tier ($29/month):**
- Unlimited AI analyses
- CSV bulk upload
- Cross-posting (coming soon)
- Priority support

**Why $29?** 
- Low enough to say yes
- High enough to be sustainable
- Industry standard for tools

**Test with sellers:** Some may say it should be $19, others $49. Listen.
